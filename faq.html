Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ltth.app

Is ltth.app free to use?

Yes! ltth.app is completely free and open source. There are no hidden costs or premium tiers.

Is it safe to use?

Absolutely. ltth.app is built with security and privacy in mind. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All operations happen locally on your device.

Which browsers are supported?

ltth.app supports Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best experience.

Can I download videos without watermarks?

Yes, ltth.app includes a watermark removal feature that allows you to download clean videos in high quality.

Does ltth.app work on mobile devices?

Currently, ltth.app is designed for desktop browsers. Mobile support is on our roadmap and will be available in future updates.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

You can report bugs or request features through our GitHub Issues page or contact us through our support page.

Can I contribute to the project?

Yes! ltth.app is open source and we welcome contributions. Check out our GitHub repository for contribution guidelines.

Will this get me banned from TikTok?

ltth.app operates within TikTok's publicly available features and doesn't violate their terms of service. However, use discretion and always respect content creators' rights.
