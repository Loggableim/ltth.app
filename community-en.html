Community Guidelines

For a friendly and productive environment

  • Be respectful: Treat others as you want to be treated. No insults or hate speech.
  • Be helpful: Share your knowledge and support others with their problems.
  • Stay constructive: Criticism is welcome as long as it's constructive and contributes to improvement.
  • No spam: Keep discussions relevant and avoid repeated or inappropriate posts.
  • Respect privacy: Don't share personal information of others without their consent.
